Using the MealPlanner tool is easy!

  • Step 1: Set up a new mealplan using our template.
  • Step 2: If you want to build a pool of cooks and/or recipes to populate your meal plan with, you can add cooks and add recipes to your plan.
  • Step 3: Now the cooks in your group can sign up to cook meals and select or add the menu items for each meal.
  • Need to delete something? No problem! You can delete a mealplan on the signup page, view and/or delete cook information here, and view and/or delete recipe information here.
  • Need more help? Access our full user's guide here.
